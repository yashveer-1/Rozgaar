The Livelihood Trust Gap

02

⚠️ The Challenge

  • Lack of Documentation: Working daily jobs generates no salary slips, HR letters, or official records.
  • No Access to Credit: Traditional banks request credit scores which informal workers lack due to cash wages.
  • Middlemen & Exploitation: Workforce deployment depends heavily on agents/brokers taking huge cuts.

✨ The Opportunity

  • Verified Livelihood Record: Convert everyday transactions and peer recommendations into a digital portfolio.
  • Dynamic Analytics: Visualise cash flows and verify income proofs using OCR scanning technology.
  • Democratised Matching: Enable direct recruitment platforms using AI matching metrics.

Smart Document Vault & OCR

06

The document vault uses Tesseract OCR in the backend node service to parse transaction screenshots (such as GPay, PhonePe, or PayTM) and automatically extract data elements.

🔍 Extracted Fields

Amount: Scans for numbers prefixing ₹, Rs., or INR.

UTR/Ref No: Matches unique 12-digit numbers to verify transactions.

Employer Name: Analyzes text structures ("Paid to...", "From...") to assign client records.

Instant OCR Dropzone

Drop UPI screenshots, salary statements, or trade certificates for auto-processing.

AI Job Matchmaking Score

07

🎯 Score Breakdown System

  • Skills Match (50%): Tracks ratio of worker's verified skills to employer prerequisites.
  • Location Proximity (20%): Full points for same-city / remote, partial points for same-state match.
  • Experience Verification (15%): Checks if experience meets job needs.
  • Salary Convergence (15%): Validates expectation limits.

The Scoring Engines

10

🛡️ Trust Score Formula

Replaces typical identity background checks using verifiable peer Vouching and transactions:

  • Verified Employer Relations (25%)
  • Verified Document Vault (20%)
  • Verified Payment History (20%)
  • Profile Completion Progress (15%)
  • Verified Skills (10%)
  • Verified References (10%)

💳 Financial Readiness Index

Evaluates stability and transaction history to determine suitability for micro-loans:

  • Income consistency rating (30%)
  • Employer verification count (20%)
  • Verified ledger payments (20%)
  • Count of verified documents (15%)
  • Employment duration stability (15%)
